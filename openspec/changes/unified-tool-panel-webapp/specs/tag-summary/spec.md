## REMOVED Requirements

### Requirement: TAG 統計 menu entry
**Reason**: The menu collapses to a single `開啟面板` item (see modified `custom-menu`).
**Migration**: Open `交易工具 → 開啟面板` and switch to the TAG tab.

### Requirement: Per-TAG spend totals
**Reason**: Folded into the `tool-panel` TAG tab.
**Migration**: Use the TAG tab in the panel — same per-TAG totals, sorted desc.

### Requirement: All-time / current-month scope toggle
**Reason**: Provided by the shared `tool-panel` scope toggle across both tabs.
**Migration**: Use the 全部/當月 toggle in the panel.

### Requirement: Drill from a TAG into its transactions
**Reason**: Folded into the `tool-panel` drill-in flow.
**Migration**: Click a TAG row in the panel to see its transactions.
